Airbnb Max Price using Tableau


The dataset is taken from the Airbnb website, specifically the listings.csv file of New York City, NY (May 6, 2024), which describes the listing activity of New York City AirBnB's. The visualization focuses on geographic location, room type and price. The business question is: which neighborhood and room type has the highest price in New York?

Tableau is used to create the visualization with the horizontal axis representing neighborhoods in New York City and the vertical axis representing the average price. The room type is color coded on the chart. To answer the business question, the neighborhood and room type with the highest price in New York City is Fifteenth Ward, Entire Home and Appartment at $358.60.
